History of TechCon

TechCon was founded in 2005 as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts and has since evolved into one of the premier global technology conferences. Over the years, we have welcomed industry leaders and innovators to discuss the future of technology.

Our Mission

TechCon aims to inspire and educate tech professionals, entrepreneurs, and enthusiasts by showcasing cutting-edge innovations and fostering a community of collaboration and innovation.

Notable Past Speakers

Elon Musk

Elon Musk, the visionary CEO of SpaceX and Tesla, shared insights on the future of space travel and renewable energy at TechCon 2018.

Sheryl Sandberg

Sheryl Sandberg, COO of Meta (formerly Facebook), discussed leadership in the digital era and the importance of diversity in tech at TechCon 2019.

Satya Nadella

Satya Nadella, CEO of Microsoft, explored the future of cloud computing and artificial intelligence at TechCon 2020.
